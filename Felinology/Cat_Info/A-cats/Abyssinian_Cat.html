Abyssinian Cat

Description

A short-haired domestic cat, the Abyssinian is highly cherished by owners for its cute almond-shaped eyes and overly expressive face. Earning its name from the country of Abyssinia, where it is supposed to have its roots, this alert, active and energetic feline would keep you on your toes always.

Overview

  • Origin: Ethiopia
  • Preffered Food: They would eat good quality cat food, alongside a diet rich in protein and vitamins. Choose a god quality canned food for these energetic cats.

Fun Facts:

  • The Abyssinian is thought to be one of the oldest domestic cat breeds.
  • In the adventure film One Hundred and One Dalmatians, Sergeant Tibbs was an Abyssinian cat.
