Balinese Cat

Description

An excellent combination of intelligence, humor, and cuteness, the Balinese with a pleasant personality is a long-haired variation of the Siamese, noted for their soft, and silky coat. Though named as the Balinese, they have no connection with Bali or any other Indonesian region.

Overview

  • Origin: United States of America
  • Preffered Food: These cats need a good quality cat food alongside a diet rich in protein and other nutrients. They are also fond of canned food that can be given in moderate amounts.

Fun Facts:

  • Balinese cats don't need many special considerations, as they're a healthy breed that doesn't have problems socializing.
  • Many believe that Balinese history starts as early as some Chinese tapestries.
