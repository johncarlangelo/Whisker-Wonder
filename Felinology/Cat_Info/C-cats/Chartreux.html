Chartreux

Description

The Chartreux is a very rare breed of French cats that are primarily characterized by their 'smiling' face and blue coats. This cat is very similar in appearance to the British Shorthair, and is a very old breed. They are large by size and have a stocky, muscular physique, but with short legs. Their coat is water resistant, with breaks like sheepskin. Their face is round with orange to copper eyes, erect ears, and long whiskers. Fun Facts:

  • With very fast reflexes, these lap cats make excellent hunters, and hence, they are much valued by farmers.
  • In the 1999 movie 'Stuart Little', the character 'Smokey the Alley cat' was a Chartreux.
