Cornish Rex

Description

The Cornish Rex is a domestic hairless cat with a striking wavy, curly coat that is an outcome of natural mutation. Referred to as the Greyhound due to its long and lean stature, these elegant looking cats are just reverse of their personality, with energy, affection, and intelligence being their dominant traits, also making them an owner's delight.

Overview

  • Origin: United Kingdom
  • Preffered Food: Besides a good quality cat food, balance its diet with protein, vitamins, and minerals. The Cornish Rex has little control over its feeding habits and may grab a bowl full of its favorite food within minutes after a meal. Therefore, owners need to be cautious about their food habits to avoid any hassles in future.

Fun Facts:

  • The Name of the First Cornish Rex Was Kallibunker.
  • Cornish Rexes Are Very Sensitive to Temperatures.
