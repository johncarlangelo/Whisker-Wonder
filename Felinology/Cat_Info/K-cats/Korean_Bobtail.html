Korean Bobtail

Description

The Korean Bobtail cat is a striking breed with an unusual tail that looks like it belongs to a rabbit rather than a cat. While this interesting breed is native to and commonly found in Korea and Southeast Asia, it's rather scarce elsewhere in the world and not so easy to find.

Overview

  • Origin: Korea
  • Preffered Food: Korean Bobtails do not necessitate a specialized diet. Providing them with high-protein cat food and ensuring they have access to fresh, clean water is essential. A balanced diet for a Korean Bobtail includes a combination of dry cat food to maintain dental health and wet food rich in protein.

Fun Facts:

  • The Korean Bobtail is a relatively rare breed so it may take some sleuthing skills to find one.
  • A Korean Bobtail will get along well with other pets, as long as those pets know how to act around cats.
