Norwegian Forest Cat

Description

Kind-hearted, affectionate, gentle - the Norwegian cat or skogkatt translating to forest cat, a native of Norway, is a beauty in itself. Big, strong and sturdy, these cats make for a perfect companion one could ask for.

Overview

  • Origin: Norway
  • Preffered Food: These big cats need to be fed well to maintain good health. Hence you need to provide them good quality cat food in measured amounts.

Fun Facts:

  • Norwegian forest cats are way larger than most cats-and some small dogs, for that matter.
  • With their big bodies and bushy tails, the Maine coon and the Norwegian forest cat look like cousins.
