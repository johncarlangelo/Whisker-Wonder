Siberian Cat

Description

The Siberian cats have a long and ancient tradition, finding its way in fairy tales and storybooks since time immemorial. Being Russia's national cat, these large to medium felines have a robust, athletic built, and an extremely pleasing appearance that makes it great house cats.

Overview

  • Origin: Russia
  • Preffered Food: Select a good quality cat food for your Siberian that should have a moderate mixture of a wet and dry diet. Cooked food, as well as a raw diet, can be incorporated alongside their cat food, though in consultation with a vet.

Fun Facts:

  • The Siberian cat is considered the most frost-resistant domestic cat.
  • Siberian cats need more space for their enormous tails and paws.
