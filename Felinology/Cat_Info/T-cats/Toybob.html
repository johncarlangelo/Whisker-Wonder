Toybob

Description

The ToyBob Cat, or Toy Bobtail, is the world’s smallest breed of domestic cats, and is also one of the rarest and most expensive. In this regard, it can easily be compared to its canid counterpart ‘Chihuahua’. Originating in Russia, the then USSR, this little color point feline is naturally bob-tailed, and does not grow more than a 3–4-month-old kitten. In spite of having a small size, they have sturdy, well-muscled bodies. They have a wedge-shaped head, a round snout, erect ears, and large almond-shaped eyes.

Overview

  • Origin: Russia
  • Preffered Food: General balanced cat diet is recommended, including meat, fish, vegetables, cereals, cottage cheese, yogurt etc. However, it is also advisable not to feed your bob with too much milk products, since that might boost up chances of developing lactose intolerance.

Fun Facts:

  • In Russia, a bobcat can cost as high as 150,000 rubles or approximately 9,500 pesos
  • ToyBob cat has a unique voice. Many individuals would try to speak out words or imitate sounds and calls such as a dog’s woof, etc.
